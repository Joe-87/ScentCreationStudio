Who Are We

A unique, multi-faceted
company with high
potential

Cinquième Sens is both a fragrance composition house and a world-renowned professional training organization. From their studio near the Eiffel Tower in Paris to their school in Grasse — the perfume capital of the world — they have been shaping the future of perfumery since 1976.

Their philosophy centers on awakening the senses, sharing expertise, and nurturing creativity across every level of the fragrance industry.
