Olfactive Science

The Olfactive Pyramid

The architecture of every fine fragrance — a carefully orchestrated sequence of sensory moments unfolding over time.

Top Notes

The opening impression lasting 15–30 minutes. Volatile, bright, and immediately striking — these are the notes that make the first encounter unforgettable.

Bergamot Lemon Pink Pepper Aldehydes

Heart Notes

The soul of the fragrance, emerging after 30 minutes and persisting for hours. This is the emotional core — where character and intention are most fully expressed.

Rose Absolute Jasmine Iris Geranium

Base Notes

The lasting foundation, often remaining on skin for 6–8 hours or more. Deep, resonant, and sensual — base notes are the memory that lingers long after the wearer has left the room.

Sandalwood Vetiver Ambrette Musks

How We Work

Our Studio Process

From the initial conversation to the final vial, a transparent, collaborative journey through the art of fragrance design.

01

Olfactive Brief

We begin with deep conversation — exploring your brand values, emotional associations, target audience, and preferred olfactory families. No formula is written before we truly understand your vision.

02

Concept & Palette

Our perfumers select an initial palette of raw materials and sketch multiple fragrance directions. Concept samples are presented for your sensory evaluation and feedback.

03

Formulation

The chosen direction is developed into a precise formula. Concentration, longevity, and sillage are refined through iterative sampling until the fragrance performs to the brief's requirements.

04

Final Delivery

Your complete formula, INCI documentation, and fragrance story are delivered. We remain available for future reformulation, scaling, or seasonal variations of your signature scent.
